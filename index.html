WHAT IS META-PROGRAMMING?

"A metaprogram is a program that manipulates other programs (or itself) as its data. The canonical example is a compiler.

Metaprogramming is the act of writing metaprograms."

IN SUMMARY

In Summary

  • Very little of what we call Metaprogramming is really all that magical
  • Usually just taking advantage of Ruby's open classes to add or override methods
  • You can often solve common, practical problems very elegantly with some of these techniques
  • Like any tool, they can be overused or misused.
  • And that's about it!
